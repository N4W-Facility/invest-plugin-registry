InVEST Plugins Registry
=======================

InVEST Plugins are

A registry of plugins for the Integrated Valuation of Ecosystem Services and Tradeoffs (InVEST) software.  For more information, please see the `feature announcement <https://naturalcapitalalliance.stanford.edu/news/new-feature-opens-door-custom-models-mapping-and-valuing-natures-benefits>`__
